Model agreement

vs you: mean absolute error against your hand labels (0 → 0, 1 → 10), over samples you have labeled for this filter. vs models: MAE against the mean of the other checked models on the same document, over all matched samples. Lower is closer. Unchecking a model excludes its ratings from every computed number on this page and from the mean ratings on the data page; its own rows stay visible so you can re-check it.

Overall accuracy

–
Checked models’ mean rating as a probability (mean/10), scored per sample as p when your label is 1 and 1 − p when it is 0, averaged over all samples labeled for this filter. Threshold-free; equals 1 − MAE/10. Higher is better.

Classification threshold

Mean rating of the checked models ≥ x classifies a sample as 1 (caught), below x as 0 (kept). positive accuracy: fraction of your hand-label-1 samples classified 1 — the complement is true positives leaking through. negative %: fraction of all samples classified 0 — the share of data a filter at this threshold keeps.
Sorted by disagreement: |model rating − hand label| for the selected filter, with binary hand labels 0/1 mapped to 0/10. Unlabeled or unmatched samples sort last. Click a row to expand it.
